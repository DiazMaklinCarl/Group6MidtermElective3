Hello, we are Group 6

Our thesis title is Respiratory Diagnostic System for Ambulatory Patient Using IoT

Our thesis project is developing a tool that can recognize various coughs, including those caused by asthma, pneumonia, the common cold, and other respiratory diseases. Additionally, this device features a vital sign monitoring system that we can use to further diagnose the condition of the patient. We will use previous datasets and studies conducted by other researchers to build our system and device.



Cough and Vital Sign Sensor and Diagnostic Device

Goal

Our goal is to create a device that will allow us to monitor patients and diagnose them of possible resiparoty illnesses through the analysis of their vital signs and cough patterns that is gathered using our device
